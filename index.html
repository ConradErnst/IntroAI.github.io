Introduction to AI

Hello, I’m Conrad, an AI researcher, and I’d like to welcome you to this page. Here, you’ll find a curated collection of topics, key concepts, and an overview of AI. With AI becoming increasingly mainstream, my goal was to create an open-source resource that offers a broad understanding of this transformative field. Please note that this page is not intended to be exhaustive or comprehensive. Most of the material presented here is not my original work; rather, it is a compilation of resources, including videos, research papers, and talks, sourced from various contributors. While I have not formally cited every source, I want to make it clear that I take no credit for these works. My aim is simply to provide a starting point for anyone interested in exploring AI and its applications. Enjoy your exploration!
